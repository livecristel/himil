Power Electronics

With power being at the core for any electronics system, the need for efficient, smaller form factor power systems is gaining importance. With push for indigenisation in defense systems, the need to provide a comprehensive design and test capability meeting stringent norms on performance is becoming a key to succcess.

Our design team is capable of designing complex systems meeting Mil grade requirements on operating conditions with full adherence to EMI/EMC, Vibration and thermal requirements. With form factor being a key challenge, the capability to design mechanicals meeting thermal dissipation requirements using innovative designs is part of the core competency and capability


Embedded Solutions

Digital circuitry design using FPGA is fast becoming an inseparable requirement of any electronics system. The ability to deliver a digital design with capability to develop complex RTL code, Software for system application is fast becoming a key differentiator.

Our full rounded digital design team with expertise in hardware development using FPGA, RTL coding and iOS development are capable of offering entire e2e solution covering generation of schematics, layout, prototyping, hardware bring up, RTL coding and development of software based on end user application. Our experience ranges from working on Xilinx, Intel and other major FPGA platforms delivering solutions to Defense, Automotive, Commercial and other IoT based applications

CAD

CAD is a crucial skill set in the process of design and manufacturing of any electronics system. Ability to design layout in any of the standard tools like Altium or Allegro from schematics is a competency that is mandatorily part needed as part of the design lifecycle.

Carrying out DFM/DFT along with comprehensive analysis related to signal integrity, power integrity and thermal are some of the key capabilities that our team delivers to manage and delivery high speed designs. Our team handles the CAD work as part of an integrated project to deliver designs and manufacturing for Customers in addition to delivering as a stand alone service to our Customer's electronics design teams
